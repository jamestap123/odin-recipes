Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is dope. I like spaghetti a lot, but if it doesnt have meatballs in it were gonna have an issue. But nonetheless, a nice spaghetti and meatballs is one of the finest things you can put on a plate. It is also very easy to make, so it is the perfect meal to for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large sacuepan. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender.
  2. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
